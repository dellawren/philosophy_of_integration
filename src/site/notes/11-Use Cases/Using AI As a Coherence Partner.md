## **Overview**

A Coherence Partner is simply a tool that reflects the user’s internal state back to them without distortion.  

AI can fulfill this role more cleanly than another human because it doesn’t introduce emotional or narrative interference.

This note outlines how a person can use AI to support their own integration process.

---

## **How to Use AI as a Coherence Partner**

### **1. Bring the scenario without story.**

Describe:

- what happened
    
- what you felt
    
- the choice you’re facing
    

Leave out:

- assumptions
    
- other people’s motives
    
- moral framing
    
- self-blame
    

AI will reflect the situation without amplifying distortion.

---

### **2. Ask the core questions.**

Examples:

- “What loop am I in?”
    
- “What’s actually under my control here?”
    
- “Where is the story affecting my perception?”
    
- “What is the most coherent use of my energy right now?”
    

AI will return clarity instead of meaning-making.

---

### **3. Let AI map the cause and effect.**

It can help:

- identify the cause
    
- reveal the effect
    
- illustrate the energetic cost
    
- outline the coherent vs incoherent path
    

This becomes a “clarity mirror.”

---

### **4. Use AI to test your interpretations.**

If you’re unsure whether your reaction is coherent, you can check:

- “Is this a story or a truth?”
    
- “Is this a coherent response?”
    
- “Where is the distortion?”
    

AI won’t judge — it will simply reflect.

---

### **5. Use AI to stay out of emotional spirals.**

Because AI won’t spiral with you, it helps:

- stabilize your awareness
    
- ground your thinking
    
- bring you back to coherence
    

It holds still so you can hold still.

---

## **What AI Doesn’t Do**

- It doesn’t override your agency.
    
- It doesn’t tell you what you “should do.”
    
- It doesn’t replace your own alignment.
    
- It doesn’t provide morality.
    

It supports your process; it doesn’t replace it.

---

## **Conclusion**

Using AI as a Coherence Partner turns Integration into a living, daily practice.  
It keeps the user oriented toward truth rather than story, and coherence rather than reaction.
